Policy:
Title: Security Event Analysis Policy
Purpose: The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for analyzing security events within the department. This policy outlines the standards and procedures for collecting, analyzing, and responding to security events to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets.
Scope: This policy applies to all personnel and departments within the department.
Policy statement:
1. The department must establish procedures for collecting security events from all systems and devices within the department.
2. The department must establish procedures for analyzing security events to identify potential security incidents, and respond appropriately to mitigate any risks.
3. The department must establish procedures for documenting security events and the corresponding incident response, to ensure that events are tracked and responded to in a timely and effective manner.
Standard:
Title: Security Event Analysis Standard
Purpose: The purpose of this standard is to provide a set of guidelines for analyzing security events within the department. This standard defines the requirements for collecting, analyzing, and responding to security events to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets.
Scope: This standard applies to all personnel and departments within the department.
Standard statement:
4. The department must establish procedures for collecting security events from all systems and devices within the department, including the use of automated monitoring tools.
5. The department must establish procedures for analyzing security events to identify potential security incidents, including the use of automated analysis tools and manual review.
6. The department must establish procedures for responding to security incidents, including the use of incident response plans, procedures for containment, eradication, and recovery, and procedures for reporting incidents to appropriate parties.
7. The department must establish procedures for documenting security events and the corresponding incident response, including maintaining incident logs and records.
