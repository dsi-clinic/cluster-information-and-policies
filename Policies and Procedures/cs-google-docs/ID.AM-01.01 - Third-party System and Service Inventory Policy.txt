Third-party System and Service Inventory Policy
Purpose
 This policy aims to ensure the maintenance of an inventory of third-party systems and services in the CS Department of the University. This policy outlines the standards and procedures for inventory management to ensure the security, availability, and performance of third-party systems and services.
Scope
This policy applies to all CS Department personnel, including faculty, staff, students, and third-party service providers with access to or managing third-party systems and services.
Policy Statement
1. The CS Department must keep an inventory of all third-party systems and services used in its operations.
2. The inventory must be accurate, up-to-date, and complete, including information such as system name, version, vendor, location, purpose, and criticality level.
3. The CS Department must perform periodic inventory reviews to ensure that it is accurate and current.
4. Any changes or updates to the inventory must be documented and communicated to relevant stakeholders.
5. The CS Department must ensure that third-party systems and services are evaluated for security, availability, and performance before they are added to the inventory.
6. The CS Department must ensure that all third-party systems and services in the inventory comply with relevant University policies and standards.
7. The CS Department must ensure that all third-party service providers comply with relevant contractual obligations and service-level agreements.
8. The CS Department must ensure that all third-party systems and services in the inventory are adequately documented and supported, and that relevant documentation is accessible to authorized personnel.
9. The CS Department must ensure that appropriate measures are taken to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all data processed, stored, or transmitted by third-party systems and services.
