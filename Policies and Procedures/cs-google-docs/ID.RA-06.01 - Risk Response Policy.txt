Risk Response Policy
Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for developing and prioritizing responses to unit risks. This policy outlines the standards and procedures for responding to risks to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data and systems.
Scope
This policy applies to all personnel within the unit, including faculty, staff, students, and third-party service providers who have access to or manage data and systems.
Policy Statement
1. The unit must establish a process for developing and prioritizing responses to unit risks.
2. The process must be based on the risks associated with the unit's data and systems and must be aligned with industry best practices and applicable laws and regulations.
3. The unit must designate a risk response manager who is responsible for overseeing the risk response process.
4. The risk response manager must ensure that appropriate measures are taken to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data and systems based on the risks associated with each area of the unit's operations.
5. The unit must establish procedures for responding to risks, including identifying appropriate responses based on the severity of the risk and the impact on the unit's operations.
6. The unit must ensure that appropriate controls and measures are in place to address identified risks.
7. The unit must ensure that all personnel who have access to or manage data and systems are aware of the risk response process and their responsibilities for maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data and systems.
