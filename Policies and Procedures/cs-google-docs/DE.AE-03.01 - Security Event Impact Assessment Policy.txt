Policy:
Title: Security Event Impact Assessment Policy
Purpose: The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for assessing the impact of security events in the CS Department. This policy outlines the standards and procedures for identifying and assessing the impact of security events to determine the severity of the incident and to guide the appropriate incident response.
Scope: This policy applies to all personnel and departments within the CS Department.
Policy statement:
1. The CS Department must establish procedures for identifying security events and determining their potential impact on information assets and operations.
2. The CS Department must establish procedures for assessing the severity of security events based on their potential impact on information assets and operations.
3. The CS Department must establish procedures for communicating the severity of security events to appropriate stakeholders, including incident response teams, management, and affected individuals.

This standard defines the requirements for identifying security events, assessing their potential impact, and communicating the severity of events to appropriate stakeholders.
Scope: This standard applies to all personnel and departments within the CS Department.

6. The CS Department must establish procedures for communicating the severity of security events to appropriate stakeholders, including incident response teams, management, and affected individuals, including the use of incident reporting tools and escalation procedures.
