Scheduling Change

New Job Scheduling System — Thursday, May 7, 2026

The cluster is moving to a 3-tier QoS system (general, protected, interactive). Jobs submitted without a tier run on general and are now preemptable — Slurm will warn you 5 minutes before killing and auto-requeue. Use --qos=protected for short guaranteed runs or --qos=interactive for development.
